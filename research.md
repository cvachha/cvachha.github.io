					<h3>WebTransceiVR (CHI 22)</h3>
				</header>
				<p>We propose WebTransceiVR, an asymmetric collaboration toolkit which when integrated into a VR application, allows multiple non-VR users to share the virtual space of the VR user. It allows external users to enter and be part of the VR applicationâ€™s space through standard web browsers on mobile and computers. WebTransceiVR also includes a cloud-based streaming solution that enables many passive spectators to view the scene through any of the active cameras. We conduct informal user testing to gain additional insights for future work.</p>
